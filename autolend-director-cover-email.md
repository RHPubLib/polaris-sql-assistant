**Subject:** Autolend kiosk — quick read

Juliane,

Short version of IT's evaluation of the Autolend holds locker for Oakland Township:

- **Can we make this work? Yes, most likely** — it integrates with our Polaris and we can secure it to our standard.
- **But it's all on the vendor's word** — every assurance is the vendor describing their own product; we have **no references or real customers we can talk to yet**.
- **The vendor agreed to everything we asked** and was candid that almost none of their sites lock it down this much — so the hardened setup would be somewhat first-time for them too.
- **One risk we can't fully remove:** it's a vendor-managed Windows device the vendor can remote into for support; we reduce that exposure but can't eliminate it.
- **A PAPI-native alternative (Lyngsoe) exists**, but its hold-locker option is just as unproven today — worth a look in a fuller comparison.
- **My recommendation: take about one more month** to get references in hand and compare a couple of other vendors before committing. It's achievable, but there's no reason to commit on vendor-word alone.
